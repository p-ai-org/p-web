      <br/>
      <br/>
      Welcome to BAILII, based at the Institute of Advanced Legal Studies,
where you can find British and Irish case law &amp; legislation,
European Union case law, Law Commission reports, and other law-related
British and Irish material.

BAILII thanks
      <a href="http://www.scottishlawreports.org.uk/">
       The Scottish
Council of Law Reporting
      </a>
      for their assistance in establishing the
      <a href="https://www.bailii.org/scot/cases/HSLR.html">
       Historic Scottish Law
Reports
      </a>
      project.

BAILII also thanks
      <a href="http://www.sentralsystems.com/">
       Sentral
      </a>
      for provision of servers and
      <a href="https://www.bytemark.co.uk/">
       Bytemark
      </a>
      for provision of hosting services.

For more information, see
      <a href="/bailii/">
       About BAILII
      </a>
      .

         </font>

        <br/>
        Usage of BAILII has increased during the pandemic. Donations are BAILII's core funding.
        <a href="https://www.bailii.org/bailii/appeal.html">
         Please consider a donation.
        </a>
        The BAILII team are working from their homes and are continuing to upload judgments as they come in; additionally, courts and tribunals are sending some older materials which they have not sent to BAILII before. The Trustees and staff of BAILII wish all users and supporters well during this difficult time.
