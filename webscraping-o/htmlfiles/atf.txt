         <h2 class="pane-title">
          About the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives
         </h2>
         <div class="fieldable-panels-pane">
          <div class="field field--name-field-basic-text-text field--type-text-long field--label-hidden">
           <div class="field__items">
            <div class="field__item even">
             <div>
              ATF is a law enforcement agency in the United StatesDepartment of Justice that protects our communities from violent criminals, criminal organizations, the illegal use and trafficking of firearms, the illegal use and storage of explosives, acts of arson and bombings, acts of terrorism, and the illegal diversion of alcohol and tobacco products. We partner with communities, industries, law enforcement, and public safety agencies to safeguard the public we serve through information sharing, training, research, and use of technology.
             </div>
