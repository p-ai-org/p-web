     <header>
      <h1 itemprop="name">
       <img alt="mass" class="logo-image" src="https://www.mass.com/images/mass-sign.png" title="mass"/>
      </h1>
     </header>
     <article itemprop="about">
      <p>
       Mass is an experience design group. Define, shape, build, and invent. Brands, digital products, IRL experiences, and ventures.
      </p>
     </article>
     <header>
      <h1 itemprop="name">
       <img alt="Stephan Valter" class="logo-image" src="https://www.mass.com/images/valter-x3.png" title="Stephan Valter"/>
      </h1>
     </header>
     <article itemprop="about">
      <p>
       Stephan Valter is the principal of Mass. Trained at the multi-disciplinary State Academy of Art and Design in Stuttgart, Germany, he opened his own design practice in New York. The German-French designer has created consumer experiences, brand identities and packaging for companies like Exxon, Coca-Cola, LVMH, and Diageo, and expanded into digital design and technology.
      </p>
     </article>
     <header>
      <h1 itemprop="name">
       <img alt="V" class="logo-image" src="https://www.mass.com/images/V-logo.png" title="V"/>
      </h1>
     </header>
     <article itemprop="about">
      <p>
       Valter's design thinking is interdisciplinary and rooted in his passion for new areas: experiences and technology, user interface and digital product design, animation and video, furniture and spatial design. His design process is holistic, connection all aspects of a brand to its core tenet.
      </p>
     </article>
     <header>
      <h1 itemprop="name">
       <img alt="Internet of Things" class="logo-image" src="https://www.mass.com/images/smrxt-spilled.png" title="Internet of Things"/>
      </h1>
     </header>
     <article itemprop="about">
      <p>
       Valter is also the co-founder and inventor of Nomi, a sensor-enabled packaging and data platform for healthcare. He is introducing the remote tracking of medication adherence, a new data category crucial for improving the health outcomes of patients with serious illnesses.
      </p>
     </article>
     <header>
      <h1 itemprop="name">
       <img alt="mass round table" class="logo-image" src="https://www.mass.com/images/mass-round-table.png" title="mass round table"/>
      </h1>
     </header>
     <article itemprop="about">
      <p>
       Mass designs the undesigned, the unidentified, the underperforming. Experiences, digital and offline, brand identity, products and packaging.
      </p>
