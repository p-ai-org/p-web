                       </span>
                       <div class="article__summary">
                        <p>
                         <strong>
                          Kansas City, MO (March 11, 2021)
                         </strong>
                          The National Association of Insurance Commissioners (NAIC) announced today that it will host
                         <a href="https://cvent.me/xk8Z1g">
                          a special screening of the film
                          <em>
                           Coded Bias
                          </em>
                          followed by a panel discussion
                          <em>
                          </em>
                          about
                          <em>
                          </em>
                          Big Data and Artificial Intelligence
                          <em>
                          </em>
                          on March 31, 2021.
                         </a>
                        </p>
                        <p>
                         The documentary
                         <em>
                          Coded Bias
                         </em>
                         follows Massachusetts Institute of Technologys Media Lab Researcher, Joy Buolamwini, as she discovers facial recognition technologys racial bias and pushes for legislation to protect people against bias in algorithms.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                         At 1:30 PM CT on March 31, 2021, Scott Kosnoff, Partner at Faegre Drinker Biddle &amp; Reath LLP, will moderate a discussion on the topic of Big Data/Artificial Intelligence. Panelists include the films director, Shalini Kantayya; North Dakota Insurance Commissioner Jon Godfread; author of
                         <em>
                          Weapons of Math Destruction
                         </em>
                         , Cathy ONeil; Birny Birnbaum from the Center for Economic Justice; and Director of Integrated Analytics, Munich RE, Hareem Naveed.
                        </p>
