     <div class="gray content-block">
      <div class="row">
       <div class="small-12 columns">
        <h2>
         Top 50 Public Research University
        </h2>
        <p>
         <span>
          Florida International University is Miami’s public research university, focused on student success. FIU is ranked a top-50 national public university by
          <em>
           Washington Monthly
          </em>
          . FIU is a top U.S. research university (R1), with more than $200 million in annual expenditures. FIU ranks 15th in the nation among public universities for patent production, which drives innovation, and is one of the institutions that helps make Florida the top state for higher education. The
          <a href="https://nexthorizon.fiu.edu/" rel="noopener noreferrer" tabindex="-1" target="_blank">
           Next Horizon
          </a>
          fundraising campaign is furthering FIU’s commitment to providing students real opportunities.
         </span>
         <span>
          Today, FIU has two campuses and multiple centers, and supports artistic and cultural engagement through its three museums:
          <a href="https://frost.fiu.edu/" rel="noopener noreferrer" tabindex="-1" target="_blank">
           Patricia &amp; Phillip Frost Art Museum
          </a>
          , the
          <a href="https://wolfsonian.org/" rel="noopener noreferrer" tabindex="-1" target="_blank">
           Wolfsonian—FIU
          </a>
          , and the
          <a href="http://jmof.fiu.edu/" rel="noopener noreferrer" tabindex="-1" target="_blank">
           Jewish Museum of Florida-FIU
          </a>
          . FIU is a member of
          <a href="https://fiusports.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer" tabindex="-1" target="_blank">
           Conference USA
          </a>
          , with more than 400 student-athletes participating in 18 sports. The university has awarded more than 330,000 degrees to many leaders in South Florida and beyond.
         </span>
        </p>
       </div>
      </div>
     </div>
    </article>
