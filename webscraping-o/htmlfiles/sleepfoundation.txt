      <h3>
       Common Sleep Disorders
      </h3>
     </div>
     <ul>
      <li>
       <a href="/insomnia">
        <div class="img-with-head">
         <figure>
          <img alt="Insomnia" class="lazy" data-srcset="https://www.sleepfoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/home-insom-288x305.jpg 300w, https://www.sleepfoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/home-insom-406x430.jpg"/>
         </figure>
         <h2>
          Insomnia
         </h2>
        </div>
        <div class="bot-content">
         <i>
          Insomnia is a sleep disorder where you have trouble falling or staying asleep. Insomnia may be short-lived (acute), or long-lasting (chronic).
         </i>
        </div>
       </a>
      </li>
      <li>
       <a href="/sleep-apnea">
        <div class="img-with-head">
         <figure>
          <img alt="Sleep Apnea" class="lazy" data-srcset="https://www.sleepfoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/woman-sleeping-homepage-287x305.jpg 300w, https://www.sleepfoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/woman-sleeping-homepage-405x430.jpg"/>
         </figure>
         <h2>
          Sleep Apnea
         </h2>
        </div>
        <div class="bot-content">
         <i>
          Marked by abnormal breathing at night, sleep apnea is one of the most common sleep disorders, affecting adults and children alike.
         </i>
        </div>
       </a>
      </li>
      <li>
       <a href="/narcolepsy">
        <div class="img-with-head">
         <figure>
          <img alt="Narcolepsy" class="lazy" data-srcset="https://www.sleepfoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/narcolepsy-illustration-homepage-287x305.jpg 300w, https://www.sleepfoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/narcolepsy-illustration-homepage-405x430.jpg"/>
         </figure>
         <h2>
          Narcolepsy
         </h2>
        </div>
        <div class="bot-content">
         <i>
          Falling asleep during the day despite eight hours of sleep? Narcolepsy, a sleep disorder, may be to blame
         </i>
        </div>
       </a>
      </li>
      <li>
       <a href="/shift-work-disorder">
        <div class="img-with-head">
         <figure>
          <img alt="Shift Work Disorder" class="lazy" data-srcset="https://www.sleepfoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/shift-work-disorder-illustration-homepage-287x305.jpg 300w, https://www.sleepfoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/shift-work-disorder-illustration-homepage-405x430.jpg"/>
         </figure>
         <h2>
          Shift Work Disorder
         </h2>
        </div>
        <div class="bot-content">
         <i>
          Many people have jobs that require them to work when they may otherwise be sleeping, resulting in poor sleep hygiene.
         </i>
        </div>
       </a>
      </li>
      <li>
       <a href="/excessive-sleepiness">
        <div class="img-with-head">
         <figure>
          <img alt="Excessive Sleepiness" class="lazy" data-srcset="https://www.sleepfoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/excessive-sleepiness-illustration-homepage-234x305.jpg 300w, https://www.sleepfoundation.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/excessive-sleepiness-illustration-homepage-330x430.jpg"/>
         </figure>
         <h2>
          Excessive Sleepiness
         </h2>
        </div>
        <div class="bot-content">
         <i>
          People struggling with excessive sleepiness feel sluggish and groggy throughout the day, which can lead to accidents and long-term health effects.
         </i>
        </div>
       </a>
      </li>
     </ul>
