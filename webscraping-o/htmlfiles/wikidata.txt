          <div class="mp-frame-header" style="background: #339966;">
           Welcome!
          </div>
          <div class="mp-frame-body">
           Wikidata is a free and open knowledge base that can be read and edited by both humans and machines.
           <p>
            Wikidata acts as central storage for the
            <b>
             structured data
            </b>
            of its Wikimedia sister projects including Wikipedia, Wikivoyage, Wiktionary, Wikisource, and others.
           </p>
           Wikidata also provides support to many other sites and services beyond just Wikimedia projects! The content of Wikidata is
           <a class="external text" href="https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/" rel="nofollow">
            available under a free license
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wikidata:Database_download" title="Special:MyLanguage/Wikidata:Database download">
            exported using standard formats
           </a>
           , and
           <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wikidata:Data_access#How_can_I_get_data_out_of_Wikidata?" title="Special:MyLanguage/Wikidata:Data access">
            can be interlinked to other open data sets
           </a>
           on the linked data web.
