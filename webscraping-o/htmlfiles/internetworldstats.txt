            <font color="#0000CC" face="Arial">
             Internet World
Stats
            </font>
           </h1>
           <p>
            <font color="#0000CC" face="Arial">
             Internet World Stats is an
International website that features up to date World Internet
Users, Population Statistics, Social Media Stats and Internet
Market Research Data, for over
             <b>
              250
             </b>
             individual countries and world
regions. See the
            </font>
            <a href="stats.htm">
             <font face="Arial">
              <b>
               Internet
Big Picture
              </b>
             </font>
            </a>
            <font color="#0000CC" face="Arial">
             here.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Internet World Stats is a useful source for country and regional
stats (statistics), international online market research, the
latest Internet information, world Internet penetration data, world
population statistics, telecommunications information reports, and
Facebook Stats by country. To clarify, the word "
             <b>
              stats
             </b>
             " is the
abbreviation of the English word statistics.
             <b>
              Statistics
             </b>
             is
defined as the collection, organization and interpretation of
numerical data.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Web site navigation and usability have been made easy with a link
on the right side of the top menu of all pages to the footer
giving access to the
             <a href="links.htm">
              <font face="Arial" size="3">
               <b>
                website content menu
               </b>
              </font>
             </a>
             . Additionally,
there are content menus on the left hand side column of most
pages. This site also offers useful links to world travel and
hotel information, telecommunications country reports, country
statistics sources, country local directories, world population
data and market research information. For a complete description
of the site contents and surfing tips, visit the
            </font>
            <a href="surfing.htm" target="_blank">
             <font face="Arial">
              <b>
               Site
Surfing Guide
              </b>
             </font>
            </a>
           </p>
