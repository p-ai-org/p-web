       <p>
        The MediaWiki software is used by
        <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Sites_using_MediaWiki" title="Special:MyLanguage/Sites using MediaWiki">
         tens of thousands of websites
        </a>
        and
        <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/MediaWiki_testimonials" title="Special:MyLanguage/MediaWiki testimonials">
         thousands of companies and organizations
        </a>
        .
It powers Wikipedia and also this website.
MediaWiki helps you collect and organize knowledge and make it available to people.
It's powerful,
        <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Localisation" title="Special:MyLanguage/Localisation">
         multilingual
        </a>
        ,
        <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/FLOSS" title="w:FLOSS">
         free and open
        </a>
        , extensible, customizable, reliable, and free of charge.
        <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Manual:What_is_MediaWiki%3F" title="Special:MyLanguage/Manual:What is MediaWiki?">
         Find out more
        </a>
        and
        <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Manual:Deciding_whether_to_use_a_wiki_as_your_website_type" title="Special:MyLanguage/Manual:Deciding whether to use a wiki as your website type">
         if MediaWiki is right for you
        </a>
        .
