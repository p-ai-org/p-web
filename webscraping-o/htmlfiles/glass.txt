   </h1>
   <p>

    Knowledge
   </h2>
   <p>
    Glass.com has deep ties to the glass industry dating back nearly 50 years. Glass.com is a sister company to Key Media and Research, which has been a provider of information services including trade magazines for the glass industry for more than 25 years and also publishes statistical reports for the industry. These roots in reliable, high-quality journalism have transferred into Glass.com's informational blog, which can be found on Info.glass.com. The blog unbiasedly answers common consumer questions about glass and glass replacement. It covers subjects ranging from auto glass Windshield Repair vs. Replacement, to 7 Ways to Finance New Windows and Doors, to Picking the Perfect Vanity Mirror, Recycling Glass Bottles, and many other topics in between.
   </p>
   <p>
    One of Glass.com's primary missions is to educate you, the consumer, so that you have the information to know what options are available and what questions to ask of glass replacement companies so you can ensure they offer high-quality service. As the adage goes, knowledge is power.
   </p>
   <h2 class="u-text-center u-push-bottom">
    Quality
   </h2>
   <p>
    Speaking of high-quality service, Glass.com works with its affiliate companies to offer top-tier service. Most of our auto glass service providers are members of the Auto Glass Safety Council™ (AGSC) which is dedicated to the safe replacement of automotive glass. It develops safety standards and trains auto glass technicians on these safe practices. Whether auto glass, windows and doors, or any other type of glass, many of our other affiliates also warranty their work against defects and are always happy to answer additional questions you may have about the glass replacement process.
   </p>
   <h2 class="u-text-center u-push-bottom">
    Convenience
   </h2>
   <p>
    Glass.com allows you to compare multiple companies and multiple price quotes with just a few easy clicks. The companies you see listed in your search results will always be companies that service your local area. Not only will you be able to preview price estimates in many cases, but you'll also be able to view each company's profile. Keep in mind, price isn't always the most important factor. You'll be able to make your decision based on other key elements such as hours of operation, services provided, a company description, and any special notations that set them apart from the competition. If you want more information, you can call our affiliate directly, or visit the links to their company website and social media pages.
   </p>

That's it. No endless internet searching and no calling around to multiple companies for multiple quotes. We consolidated everything into one easy to use platform.
