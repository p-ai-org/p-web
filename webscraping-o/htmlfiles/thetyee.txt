                Many have felt their needs are
                <a href="https://thetyee.ca/News/2020/10/21/NDP-Back-To-School-Roll-Out-Divides-Polls/">
                 not being accommodated
                </a>
                , and
                <a href="https://thetyee.ca/News/2020/11/09/Parents-Share-Doubts-Facebook-COVID-Schools/">
                 parent-teacher networks
                </a>
                have arisen to follow outbreaks in schools. Parents have highlighted the
                <a href="https://thetyee.ca/News/2020/05/20/Home-Schooling-Four-Parents/">
                 difficulties of remote learning
                </a>
                for
                <a href="https://thetyee.ca/Opinion/2020/06/30/Remote-Learning-Failure-For-Family/">
                 their families
                </a>
                , and some have faced
                <a href="https://thetyee.ca/News/2020/08/12/Open-Schools-Vulnerable-Families/">
                 tough choices
                </a>
                about sending their kids back to school.
               </p>
               <p>
                Educators have wondered how
                <a href="https://thetyee.ca/Analysis/2020/06/03/Pandemic-Where-Real-Learning-Happens/">
                 to adapt
                </a>
                their teaching to a
                <a href="https://thetyee.ca/Analysis/2020/08/31/Five-Things-Before-BC-Schools-Reopen/">
                 pandemic environment
                </a>
                , from
                <a href="https://thetyee.ca/News/2020/05/11/A-BC-Teachers-Pandemic-Diary/">
                 remote learning
                </a>
                to
                <a href="https://thetyee.ca/News/2020/04/20/Beyond-Home-School-Is-A-Pandemic-The-Time-To-Unschool/">
                 self-directed education
                </a>
                to
                <a href="https://thetyee.ca/Analysis/2020/05/13/Pandemic-Should-Change-Education/">
                 the curriculum
                </a>
                . Kids'
                <a href="https://thetyee.ca/News/2020/11/13/Kid-During-COVID-Not-Good/">
                 long-term mental health
                </a>
                is a growing concern.
               </p>
               <p>
                Deep dive to learn more from parents, teachers, students and health and education experts.
               </p>
